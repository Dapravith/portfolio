# Portfolio Assets Setup

## Required Files:

### 1. Profile Photo
- **File name:** `profile.jpg`
- **Location:** `/public/profile.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 800x800 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Notes:** This will be displayed as a circular image in the hero section

### 2. Resume PDF
- **File name:** `resume.pdf`
- **Location:** `/public/resume.pdf`
- **Format:** PDF
- **Notes:** This will be downloaded when users click the "Download Resume" button

## How to Add Your Files:

1. Add your profile photo to `/Users/rothadapravith/Desktop/portfolio/public/` and name it `profile.jpg`
2. Add your resume PDF to `/Users/rothadapravith/Desktop/portfolio/public/` and name it `resume.pdf`
